## Priority 1: Must-Have for Core Framework (M-00)

### 1. Sonnenburg — Disappearing Microbiota / Hadza Studies
- **What:** Evidence that traditional populations (Hadza, Yanomami) retain ancestral microbial species lost in industrialized populations
- **Why:** Establishes that microbiome loss IS ancestral encoding loss — not metaphorical
- **Search terms:** Sonnenburg disappearing microbiota, Hadza microbiome, Smits seasonal cycling
- **Status:** Not yet crawled

### 2. HDAC Isoform Specificity of SCFAs
- **What:** Do butyrate, propionate, and acetate inhibit different HDAC isoforms? Is the epigenetic signal species-specific?
- **Why:** Core to the argument that different species = different encoding programs (not generic "gut health")
- **Search terms:** butyrate HDAC specificity, SCFA histone deacetylase isoform, propionate HDAC Class I
- **Status:** Not yet crawled

### 3. Atarashi — Clostridium Treg Induction
- **What:** Specific Clostridium species inducing regulatory T cells in the colon
- **Why:** Direct evidence of microbiome → immune education (Defense operation encoding)
- **Search terms:** Atarashi Clostridium Treg induction, colonic regulatory T cells microbiota
- **Status:** Not yet crawled
