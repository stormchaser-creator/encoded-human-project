## Debate

### Against Hypothesis A
The 80/20 anatomical ratio may not translate to 80/20 functional weight. A single well-timed efferent command can regulate millions of effector cells; afferent signals may be quantitatively dominant but functionally preparatory. Clinical transcutaneous auricular VNS, which activates both afferent and efferent fibers, does improve HRV — but so does cardiac pacing, which is purely efferent. The hypothesis that afferent is *primary* requires more than the 80/20 ratio to establish.

**Defense:** The clinical evidence for somatic, bottom-up interventions (EMDR, SE, breathing) outperforming top-down cognitive approaches for autonomic dysregulation in trauma populations is substantial. The question may be less 'afferent vs. efferent' and more 'which is the rate-limiting step' — and the evidence consistently points to afferent restoration as the bottleneck.

### Against Hypothesis B
The fractal mirror entries are authored within a single integrated framework. Their structural similarity may reflect authorial consistency rather than empirical convergence. This is not multi-source evidence — it is one conceptual system applied at three scales. The soul and spirit mirrors could be sophisticated metaphor rather than independent discovery.

**Defense:** The convergence between polyvagal theory, attachment theory, and non-dual contemplative traditions occurred in the literature *before* this framework was constructed, documented by Schore (2019), Porges (2011), and Varela (1991) independently. The mirrors may be accurately reflecting pre-existing convergence rather than inventing it.

### Against Hypothesis C
Schumann resonance (7.83 Hz) and biological rhythm coupling lacks established mechanistic transduction. Field strengths are extremely low. The cholinergic anti-inflammatory pathway is fully explained by neural-chemical signaling without requiring EM field involvement. Adding EM coupling increases explanatory complexity without increasing predictive specificity.

**Defense:** The hypothesis is appropriately tier 3 — speculative but falsifiable. The internal phase-coupling component (cardiac-respiratory RSA) is not speculative; only the environmental extension is. The hypothesis correctly identifies that HRV is a phase-coherence metric even if the environmental entrainment claim remains unproven.
