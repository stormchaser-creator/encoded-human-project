## Cross-Guild Patterns

### The Ancestral Signature
High: Prevotella, F. prausnitzii, Bifidobacterium, Roseburia, Akkermansia
Low: Enterobacteriaceae (pathobionts), Clostridioides difficile
**Interpretation:** Intact ancestral encoding — fiber-adapted, barrier-competent, immune-educated

### The Western Collapse
High: Bacteroides (monoculture dominance), Proteobacteria, Enterobacteriaceae
Low: Prevotella, F. prausnitzii, Bifidobacterium, Akkermansia
**Interpretation:** Ancestral encoding eroded — reception narrowed, defense compromised, regulation dysregulated

### The Post-Antibiotic Desert
Low: Everything — diversity collapse
High: Opportunistic fungi (Candida), C. difficile
**Interpretation:** Ancestral encoding wiped — requires intentional recolonization (Layer 8: Chosen encoding)
