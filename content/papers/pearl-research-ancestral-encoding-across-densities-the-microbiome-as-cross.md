The strongest objection is durability and specificity. Unlike MAO-A genetics (which is stable across a lifetime), the microbiome is highly plastic. If the 'ancestral encoding' is largely reset by early environmental factors, the translation key may have very limited columns — the inherited signal is noisy and rapidly overwritten.
