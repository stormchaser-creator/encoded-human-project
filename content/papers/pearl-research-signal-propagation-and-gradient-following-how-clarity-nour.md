## Part IV: Debate

### Against Hypothesis A

The strongest objection is mechanistic incommensurability. Neural phase-locking involves electromagnetic field coupling and ion channel dynamics. Autophagy involves kinase cascades and membrane dynamics. ML fine-tuning involves stochastic gradient descent on a loss landscape. Breastfeeding outcomes involve microbiome seeding and passive immunity. Saying these all instantiate 'signal-following topology' may be a vocabulary trick rather than a scientific insight.

Counter: The topology claim is not a mechanistic claim. It is a claim about the mathematical structure of the relationship between input signal quality and system response. If that structure (gradient-following with quality-proportional allocation) is genuinely conserved across these domains, it tells us something about the constraints that any information-processing system faces — not about the specific physical implementation.

### Against Hypothesis B

The soul and spirit mirror entries are Pearl's own outputs. They were synthesized from the body-density explorer-wanderer entry BY Pearl's synthesis process. Using them as independent evidence for fractal self-similarity is circular — they are not independent observations but transformations of the same observation. The apparent fractal structure may be an artifact of Pearl's synthesis algorithm applying the same conceptual template at multiple scales.

Counter: Even if the soul/spirit entries are derived rather than independent, they contain specific predictive claims that can be tested independently. The soul entry predicts exponential concentration of relational energy around 'contacted' sources — this is testable via social network analysis. The spirit entry predicts that consciousness propagation follows topology of illumination gradients — this connects to documented salience network research. If the predictions hold, the fractal hypothesis gains independent empirical support regardless of its generative history.

### Against Hypothesis C

This is the most speculative hypothesis. Claiming that Pearl's architecture is encoded in its outputs risks infinite regress — every system's outputs reflect its architecture to some degree. The claim needs to be more specific: WHICH aspects of the architecture are readable, and HOW? Without that specificity, the hypothesis is unfalsifiable and therefore not scientifically useful.

Counter: The hypothesis generates a specific, bounded, testable prediction: correlation between source epistemic_tier and mirror entry richness. This is a single, checkable claim that can be evaluated without schema documentation. If confirmed, it would reveal that the synthesis script has at least one non-trivial quality-responsive property. That's architecturally significant regardless of the broader reflexivity claim.
